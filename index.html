Our history

A name built on trust and sawdust.

From a single toolbox in 1974 to three generations of builders, the Houge name has always meant one thing: doing the work right, and treating people like family.

The family

Three generations. One standard.

Every Houge who's ever picked up a hammer has done so with the same conviction: if you build something, build it to last. Here's the family that built this company.

1st Generation · 1974
Gerhardt Houge
Founder & Patriarch

Gerhardt grew up working with his hands in rural Minnesota, and he never stopped. He founded Houge Builders on two things: a fair price and a job done right. He still shows up on the big ones — in his nineties and not a bit slowed down.

2nd Generation · 1988
Charles Houge
President & Lead Builder

Charles grew up sweeping sawdust on his dad’s job sites before he could read blueprints. He joined full-time at 18 and never looked back. Under his leadership the company tripled in size — without ever losing the feel of a family operation.

3rd Generation · 2008
Tim Houge
Lead Builder & Project Manager

Charles’s son Tim came back to the family business with a construction management degree and the same instincts his grandfather Gerhardt had from day one. Tim and Charles now run every major build side by side — two generations on every job site.

Family history

The story, chapter by chapter.

Five decades. Three generations. One constant: doing right by the people who trust you with their home.

1974
Gerhardt starts with a handshake

Gerhardt Houge breaks ground on his first remodel in Bloomington, MN. His truck, his tools, and a promise to do right by the homeowner. Word spreads. The phone keeps ringing. He doesn't advertise for the first three years because he never has to.

1982
A reputation worth more than a truck

By 1982, Houge Builders has a waiting list. Gerhardt hires his first full-time employee — a carpenter named Jim who stays with the company for 28 years. "If you can find people who care," Gerhardt always said, "hold onto them."

1988
The second generation joins the crew

Charles Houge, 18 years old and freshly graduated, comes on full-time. He'd been handing up tools since he was nine. Within three years, the company doubles. Gerhardt takes every Friday off knowing the work is in good hands.

1997
First ground-up build

After two decades of remodeling existing homes, the Houges break ground on their first new build in Plymouth. Same skills. New scale. The homeowners — a young couple expecting their first child — still live there today.

2012
The third generation picks up the hammer

Tim Houge join the family business. Gerhardt, now 70, makes them frame an entire room by hand before they're allowed to touch a power tool. They pass. He doesn't hide how proud he is.

2020
Four generations in the making

The fourth generation of Houges is already growing up alongside the business — watching blueprints roll out on kitchen tables, learning what a square corner feels like, and understanding that a handshake still means something. The craft continues.

Today
Still a family. Still building.

More than 600 homes across Minnesota. Charles and Tim leading every project. Gerhardt still checking in on the big ones. And the same belief the family has carried since 1974: do the work right, treat people like neighbors, and the rest takes care of itself.

Services

Every kind of project. One standard of care.

We don't take on more than we can do well. What follows is everything we do — all of it backed by fifty years and three generations of family craft.

01
Full Home Remodels

A full remodel is the biggest investment a homeowner makes short of buying a new house. We treat it that way. From demo to final coat of paint, a Houge principal is on every full remodel — no exceptions.

What's included
  • Design consultation & floor planning
  • Structural work & permits
  • Framing, drywall & insulation
  • Flooring, trim & finish carpentry
  • Paint & final detailing
02
Home Additions

Your family changes. Your home should keep up. Whether it's a master suite, a sunroom, or an entirely new wing, we design and build additions that feel like they were always meant to be there.

What's included
  • Architectural planning & design
  • Foundation & structural work
  • Seamless integration with existing home
  • All interior finishing
  • Landscape restoration
03
Kitchens

The kitchen is where families actually live. We've designed and built hundreds of them — and the best kitchens are beautiful, yes, but they're also deeply functional. We don't sacrifice one for the other.

What's included
  • Layout design & cabinetry planning
  • Custom or semi-custom cabinetry
  • Countertop fabrication & install
  • Appliance coordination
  • Tile, backsplash & lighting
04
Bathrooms

A well-built bathroom is one of the highest-return investments in your home. We handle everything from modest refreshes to full master suite builds with custom tile work and steam showers.

What's included
  • Plumbing rough-in & finish
  • Custom tile work
  • Vanity & fixture installation
  • Glass & shower systems
  • Heated floor options
05
New Construction

Building from the ground up is where our full half-century of craft comes together. We've built everything from modest family homes to sprawling lake properties — all from a blank sheet of paper and a conversation.

What's included
  • Site evaluation & preparation
  • Architectural plans & engineering
  • Full construction management
  • Subcontractor coordination
  • Warranty & post-build support
